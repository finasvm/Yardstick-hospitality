About yardstick

Our Profile

The story of Yardstick begins with Bipin Venugopal. Bipin entered the hospitality industry at the Leela Kempenski Hotels, Palaces, and Resorts over two decades ago.

Over time, he has worked with big chains at top management roles such as Hyatt Hotel Corporation, Alila Hotels and Resorts, ITC Hotels, Resorts, and Palaces Hilton Worldwide in Seychelles, making his way up to General Manager.

He also had an enriching stint in the United States, handling the operations for the Carnival Cruise Line.

Bipin was instrumental in several successful preopening projects as well, bringing unparalleled experience and expertise required to transform a concept into reality.

Having experienced food and hospitality in all its flavors and colors, Bipin launched his venture, Salt Mango Tree, with his company White Canvas Hospitality in 2014. Today, it is ranked Zomato's no.1 Kerala cuisine restaurant in Bangalore, with three restaurants across the city.

He realized that his vast expertise in building a hospitality brand could help many others as well. He assembled some of his colleagues that he met through his stints worldwide, and that is how we came into existence.

Since then, we have helped many clients achieve their dreams by developing them into stand-out brands.
